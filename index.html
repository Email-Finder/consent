A tiny two-step consent helper

  1. Include <script src="consent.js"></script> anywhere inside your <head>.
  2. Update analytics tags to type="text/plain" and add data-category="analytics". 
<script src="consent.js"></script>
<script type="text/plain" data-category="analytics" src="gtag.js"></script>
