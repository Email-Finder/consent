Consent demo

This page follows the exact two-step setup shown in the README.

  1. Include <script src="consent.js"></script> in your <head>.
  2. Mark every analytics tag with type="text/plain" and data-category="analytics".
<script src="consent.js"></script>
<script type="text/plain" data-category="analytics" src="https://example.com/gtag.js"></script>

Open the console to watch the analytics block run only after you accept.
